Privacy Policy for E1RM Calculator

Last updated: April 15, 2026

Overview

E1RM Calculator ("the App") is available on Android and iOS and is designed with your privacy as a priority. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use either version of the App.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

The App performs all calculations locally on your device. We do not:

  • Collect or store your workout data
  • Require account creation or login
  • Track your usage or behaviour
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Share any data with third parties
  • Transmit any information off your device

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App saves your preferences (units of measurement, weight rounding settings) and your purchase status (whether you have supported the developer) to your device's local storage. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to any server
  • Is not accessible to the developer or any third party
  • Can be cleared at any time by uninstalling the App

All values you enter (weights, reps, RPE) exist only temporarily in memory while the App is running. When you close the App, entered values are cleared.

In-App Purchases

The App offers an optional one-time in-app purchase ("Support Developer") to support development. Purchases are processed entirely by the platform store:

  • Android: processed by Google Play in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.
  • iOS: processed by the Apple App Store in accordance with Apple's Privacy Policy.

The developer does not collect, store, or process any payment or billing information. The App only receives confirmation of whether the purchase was completed.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics platforms, advertising networks, or data services. The only third-party interaction is the platform's own billing service for the optional in-app purchase described above.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since no data is collected or transmitted, the App is safe for users of all ages.

Security

Since the App does not collect, store, or transmit any user data externally, there are no security concerns related to data breaches or unauthorised access to personal information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We recommend reviewing this page periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact the developer via GitHub: github.com/gooseyirl.

Your Consent

By using the App, you consent to this privacy policy.

Summary

E1RM Calculator is a privacy-focused application that:

  • Does NOT collect any personal data
  • Does NOT track your usage
  • Does NOT share any information with third parties
  • Does NOT process payment data (handled entirely by Google Play or the Apple App Store)
  • Stores only your app preferences and purchase status locally on your device
  • Performs all calculations locally on your device
